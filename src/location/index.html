Location

The Venue and How to get there.

Estufa Fria

  • If you've been here for our two previous editions, you know we like to keep things interesting. Following that mantra, this year LXJS is happening inside a huge Art Deco hall within Lisbon's Estufa Fria (Greenhouse).

    It will keep you fresh from Lisbon's hot summer, but you'll still be right in the center of Lisbon, walking distance from most hotels, attractions and public transport.

How to get to the venue

  • Subway

    Take the "Metro" to "Parque" (blue line) or "Marquês de Pombal" (blue and yellow line). The venue is in the park, on your left side when you walk it up from the big roundabout. Metro/subway map (PDF).

  • Bus

    There are various stations all around the venue, served by major lines. You can calculate the best route on Google Maps. Official timetables.

  • Train

    You can take the "Cascais" line towards "Cais do Sodré" station or the "Sintra" line towards "Rossio" station and then take the subway. Check the timetables.

  • Parking

    If you can't find a parking spot next to Estufa Fria, there are several parking houses closeby. Check Parkopedia for more info.
