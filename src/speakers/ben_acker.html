Speakers / Ben Acker

Ben Acker is a developer at Walmartlabs that works on Spümko libraries and uses them to implement APIs. In addition to helping out with his local Node.js community (and others), he has a healthy interest in cephalopods for drawing, nodebots for building, and apiaries for tending.
