Speakers / Alex Potsides

Alex is a web developer with experience of everything from start ups to the enterprise. A co-organiser of NodeBots of London, at the weekend he can be found mostly herding robotic cats, fleeing from laser toting rovers and wondering whether his semi-autonomous brewery achieving sentience would be a good thing or not.
