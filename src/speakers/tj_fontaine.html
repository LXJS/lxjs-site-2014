Speakers / TJ Fontaine

TJ Fontaine is the Node.js Project Lead at Joyent. He has been using Node since the 0.4 days and specializes in debugging core issues. Drawing from 14 years of open source background, TJ is serving his 5th term in the Open and Free Technology Community (OFTC).
