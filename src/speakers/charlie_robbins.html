Speakers / Charlie Robbins

Charlie Robbins is the Founder & CEO of Nodejitsu. An open source enthusiast, he the curator of EmpireJS and the author of many popular Node.js modules such as forever, winston, nconf, and node-http-proxy. Before falling in love with Node.js & Javascript he was a developer in the enterprise software world, where he worked for Microsoft and consulted at many large financial institutions.
