Speakers / Brittany Storoz

Brittany Storoz is a [mostly] front-end developer working in the newsroom of The New York Times. She hacks on all things JavaScript and you'll ocassionally find her lecturing about IIFEs and closures at startup schools. She believes in life, liberty and the pursuit of happiness and has been known to pick up pencils with her toes.
